Playing the flute was pretty much my life’s focus for the next 20 years. I practiced the flute a lot. After college graduation, I practiced more. I got a 9-5 job to pay the bills. I played my flute and taught lessons to some great kids (and adults). Stopped practicing as much. Got promoted at work. Worked too much. Grew an interest in tech. Quit my job to learn to code. Built this website from scratch. Itching to learn more and make a career as a software developer.
